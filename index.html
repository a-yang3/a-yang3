About Me

I'm currently a second year studying Cognitive Science at the University of California, Berkeley. My background encompasses neuroscience, design and coding because the intersection of the three makes me really excited. I've dabbled in Python, Swift, and html, while pretty proficient with the Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, Premiere) and wireframing applications (Sketch, Principle). I love getting my hands on new things and making stuff, some of which you can check out in my portfolio. In my free time, I make music with the UC Berkeley Symphony Orchestra and teach as part of the facilitation team for Art 198/"Art for Animation".

Relevant Coursework: The Structure and Interpretation of Computer Programs - Methods of Mathematics: Calculus, Statistics, Combinatorics - Introduction to Cognitive Science - General Chemistry - Foundations of Data Science - Brain, Mind, Behavior
